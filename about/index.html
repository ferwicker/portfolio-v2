about me

My name is Fer and I am a graphic and web designer/developer from Sydney. I was born and raised in Mexico City, and moved to Sydney in 2012. I have over 8 years' experience working in print and digital design in several industries.

I started studying Graphic Design and Marketing in 2010, and started work as a designer in the print industry in 2011. In 2012 I moved to Sydney, where I studied a Cert IV and Diploma of Graphic design at Design Centre Enmore.

Since graduating I have worked in print and digital design mainly in the hospitality industry. In 2020 I started a freelance business focused on web design for small and medium businesses. This new experience brought out a curiosity and love for development, which I have decided to focus my career on.

I am currently studying a Bootcamp of Full Stack Development with the University of Sydney and I am keen to work on new and exciting web development projects of all sizes.
